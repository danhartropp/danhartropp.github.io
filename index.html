Online campaign tools

My campaign tools started out as a simple way for organisations to get their supporters to email people in power ... generally their local MP. It was designed with input from a wide range of campaigners, who now use it to send hundreds of thousands of emails a year between them. The system now includes a petition module and can also be used to respond to consultations and even organise twitter campaigns.

If you're looking for a simple, affordable and very flexible online campaign system, click the button below.

EU Insider evolved from a popular health-sector newsletter that covered developments in the EU. We now cover dozens of topics from cultural heritage to renewable energy, tracking and filtering news from thousands of sources every day. Our subscribers get a completely free daily (or weekly) digest of the news that is most relevant to them and their jobs.

We take pride in having some of the broadest coverage of any monitoring service in the EU and some of the fanciest tech to make the system work. And we're proud to give it all away for free.

I built PoliMonitor to help track political discussions on Twitter. It started out as a simple in-house tool and now has thousands of users all over the world, and tracks tens of thousands of elected officials.

Users can find out instantly when a Parliamentarian mentions them or issues they're interested in, with translated Tweets arriving in their inbox within minutes. We also help users use this data to understand long term trends and engage policy makers more effectively.
